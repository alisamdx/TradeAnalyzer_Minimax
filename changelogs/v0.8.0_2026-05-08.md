# v0.8.0 — 2026-05-08

## Phase 8: Portfolio Tracking (Priority 6)

### Database Migration

**`006_portfolio.sql`**
- `positions` table:
  - `position_type`: CSP (Cash-Secured Put), CC (Covered Call), Stock
  - `quantity`: number of contracts or shares
  - `entry_price`, `entry_date`, `entry_notes`
  - `exit_price`, `exit_date`, `exit_notes` (NULL for open positions)
  - `strike_price`, `expiration_date`, `premium_received` (for options)
  - `current_price`, `unrealized_pnl`, `realized_pnl` (calculated)
  - `status`: 'open' | 'closed'
- Indexes: `idx_positions_ticker`, `idx_positions_status`, `idx_positions_type`
- Views:
  - `v_open_positions` - open positions with capital required and unrealized P&L
  - `v_closed_positions` - closed positions with realized metrics and days held
  - `v_portfolio_summary` - aggregated stats by position type and status
- Trigger: `trg_positions_updated_at` - auto-updates timestamp on update

### P&L Calculations

**Unrealized P&L** (Open positions):
- Stock: `(currentPrice - entryPrice) * quantity`
- Options: `premiumReceived` (simplified)

**Realized P&L** (Closed positions):
- Stock: `(exitPrice - entryPrice) * quantity`
- CSP/CC: `(exitPrice - entryPrice) * quantity * 100 + premiumReceived`

**Win Rate**: Percentage of closed positions with positive realized P&L
