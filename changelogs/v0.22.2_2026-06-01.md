## feat: Analysis — sidebar order + per-tab default sort + Options Income columns

### Sidebar
- Analysis (📊) moved above Strategy Lab (🔬) in the Strategy section

- Buy Opportunities → Score/10 desc (best score at top)
- Options Income → Premium desc (highest credit at top)

- Bullish / Bearish → Max Loss desc (largest position at top)

- Analysis service passes `contract.bid`, `contract.ask`, `contract.openInterest` through in both the success and empty-result paths
- All three new columns are sortable via the column header
